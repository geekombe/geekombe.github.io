    About Me


    Hello! I'm Victor Njogu, better known in the digital realm as Geekombe. I'm a visionary digital alchemist and a passionate advocate for the transformative power of technology in Africa. My journey has been unconventional and deeply rewarding, marked by a relentless pursuit of innovation and the democratization of technology access across the continent.

    I've never been in the corporate path yet. Instead, my career has been a thrilling expedition through a series of ambitious projects and startups that I've nurtured from the ground up. My creations, including Nexaproxy and the soon-to-launch techanics.co.ke, are testaments to my dedication to solving real-world problems through tech. I have also worked on providing accessible internet services with Techanet (formerly Urban Cellular), pay as you use hotspots.

    I'm not just a coder; I'm a builder, constantly experimenting and learning, blending self-taught knowledge with formal education from Moringa School's software engineering program. This blend of formal and self-directed learning has been the cornerstone of my growth as a tech innovator.

    Beyond building and coding, I'm a content creator passionate about raising tech awareness. Through my online presence (just Google "geekombe" to see my work), I aim to demystify technology, making it approachable and exciting for everyone, not just the tech-savvy.

    My aspirations stretch far beyond the projects I've built. I dream of a future where technology is not just big in Africa but is also deeply integrated into the fabric of daily life, celebrated and leveraged by everyone. My startups and SaaS solutions, like Nexaproxy, embody this vision by tearing down barriers like paying with crypto to simplifying transactions with M-Pesa integrations and other local banking solutions that are familiar to every one, my aim has always been to make technology user-friendly and accessible to all.

    In every line of code I write, every project I launch, and every content piece I create, my goal remains unwavering: to illuminate the path to a tech-empowered Africa. Join me on this journey, and let's explore the endless possibilities that technology holds for our continent.
